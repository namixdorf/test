Before you were a manager, your job was to get work done. Now that you’re a manager, your job is to guide and lead others to get work done. It’s not an easy skill to learn, but it’s an essential and very valuable one. Balancing leading and doing helps your associates grow as well as makes you and your team more productive and effective. It also helps you strike a manageable work life balance.

If you need help learning how to delegate, you aren’t alone. Balancing leading and doing is one of the most difficult skills for managers to master. Help is available, though. Look through this section to find useful advice and resources.
